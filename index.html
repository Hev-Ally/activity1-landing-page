Must Know

Web development is the process of creating and maintaining websites, involving front-end (client-side) and back-end (server-side) development to build functional, interactive, and visually appealing web applications. Web development is crucial because it enables businesses, organizations, and individuals to establish an online presence, reach a global audience, and provide accessible information, services, and products. It drives digital communication, e-commerce, and innovation, making it essential for modern growth and connectivity.
